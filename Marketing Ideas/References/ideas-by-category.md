# The 139 Marketing Ideas — Full Reference

Complete catalog organized by category. Use idea numbers when referencing from SKILL.md.

---

## Content & SEO (1–10)

1. **Easy Keyword Ranking** — Target low-competition, long-tail keywords competitors overlook. Fast path to organic traffic.
2. **SEO Audit** — Audit your site, document fixes publicly. Builds authority and backlinks.
3. **Glossary Marketing** — Comprehensive glossary pages targeting "what is X" searches. Each term = one SEO page.
4. **Programmatic SEO** — Template-driven pages at scale: location pages, comparison pages, integration pages.
5. **Content Repurposing** — One blog post becomes a Twitter thread, YouTube video, podcast clip, infographic.
6. **Proprietary Data Content** — Use your product's unique data for original research reports competitors can't replicate.
7. **Internal Linking** — Strategic linking builds topical clusters and distributes SEO authority across your site.
8. **Content Refreshing** — Update old content with fresh data and examples. Often outperforms new content.
9. **Knowledge Base SEO** — Optimize help docs for search. Captures users actively looking for solutions.
10. **Parasite SEO** — Publish on high-authority platforms (Medium, LinkedIn, Substack) that rank faster than your domain.

---

## Competitor & Comparison (11–13)

11. **Competitor Comparison Pages** — "[Your Product] vs [Competitor]" pages capture high-intent bottom-of-funnel searchers.
12. **Marketing Jiu-Jitsu** — Turn competitor weaknesses into your strengths (they raise prices → you run affordability campaign).
13. **Competitive Ad Research** — Use SpyFu / Facebook Ad Library to study what's working for competitors.

## Free Tools & Engineering (14–22)

14. **Side Projects as Marketing** — Small useful tools attract users who later convert to your paid product.
15. **Engineering as Marketing** — Build free calculators, analyzers, or generators that solve real problems and lead to your product.
16. **Importers as Marketing** — "Import from [Competitor]" tool reduces switching friction.
17. **Quiz Marketing** — Interactive quizzes that qualify leads while engaging users. High share rate.
18. **Calculator Marketing** — ROI calculators, pricing estimators. Attract links and rank well organically.
19. **Chrome Extensions** — Browser extensions as standalone value + distribution via Chrome Web Store.
20. **Microsites** — Focused microsites for specific campaigns or audiences. Can rank faster than main domain.
21. **Scanners** — Free scanning tools: website auditors, security checkers, performance analyzers.
22. **Public APIs** — Open APIs let developers build on your platform, creating ecosystem effects.

## Paid Advertising (23–34)

23. **Podcast Advertising** — Sponsor relevant podcasts. Host-read ads especially effective.
24. **Pre-targeting Ads** — Show awareness ads before launching direct response. Warm audiences convert better.
25. **Facebook Ads** — Detailed targeting + retargeting. Test creative variations aggressively.
26. **Instagram Ads** — Visual-first. Stories and Reels capture attention well.
27. **Twitter/X Ads** — Promoted tweets and follower campaigns for engaged professional audiences.
28. **LinkedIn Ads** — Target by job title, company size, industry. High CPM but strong B2B intent.
29. **Reddit Ads** — Reach passionate communities. Authentic, transparent messaging wins.
30. **Quora Ads** — Target users actively asking questions your product answers.
31. **Google Ads** — Capture high-intent search queries: brand, competitor, and category terms.
32. **YouTube Ads** — Pre-roll and discovery ads targeting users consuming related content.
33. **Cross-Platform Retargeting** — Consistent messaging following users across platforms.
34. **Click-to-Messenger Ads** — Ads that open direct conversations instead of landing pages.
